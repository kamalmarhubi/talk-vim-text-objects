Magic pseudo-motions

  • aw – a word
  • iw – inner word
  • as – a sentence
  • is – inner sentence
  • ap – a paragraph
  • ip – inner paragraph
  • a( – a () delimited block
  • i( – inner () delimited block
  • a{ – a {} delimited block
  • i{ – inner {} delimited block
  • a[ – a [] delimited block
  • i[ – inner [] delimited block
  • a< – a <> delimited block
  • i< – inner <> delimited block
  • at – a tag
  • it – inner tag
